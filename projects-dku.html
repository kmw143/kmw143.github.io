What is DKU Student Broadcasting?

Top Duke Kunshan University students and Kunshan community monthly news in video format. DKU Student Broadcasting is a student-led club reporting campus stories. I helped film and announce in Episode 1. Since then, I have taken role as financial officer of the club. We hope you enjoy our news stories.
